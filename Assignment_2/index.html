Top Colleges and Universities in Pune

College of Engineering, Pune (COEP)

Established in 1854, COEP is one of the oldest engineering colleges in India, known for its excellent academic curriculum and strong alumni network.

Fergusson College

Founded in 1885, Fergusson College offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in arts and science streams.

Symbiosis International University

Symbiosis is a private co-educational university known for its diverse courses, global exposure, and excellent placement opportunities.

MIT World Peace University

MIT-WPU offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs with a focus on holistic education and world peace.

Bharati Vidyapeeth University

Bharati Vidyapeeth is a deemed university offering a variety of programs in multiple disciplines including engineering, management, law, and arts.
