Historical Places of Pune

Shaniwar Wada

Shaniwar Wada is a historical fortification in the city of Pune. It was the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire until 1818.

Aga Khan Palace

The Aga Khan Palace was built in 1892 by Sultan Muhammed Shah Aga Khan III in Pune. It is a majestic building and an important landmark in Indian history.

Sinhagad Fort

Sinhagad is a hill fortress located southwest of Pune. It has been the site of many important battles, most notably the Battle of Sinhagad in 1670.

Dagadusheth Halwai Ganapati Temple

The Dagadusheth Halwai Ganapati Temple is a popular Hindu temple in Pune. It is dedicated to the Hindu God Ganesh and is a significant pilgrimage site.

Pataleshwar Cave Temple

The Pataleshwar Cave Temple is a rock-cut cave temple dating back to the 8th century. It is dedicated to the Hindu god Shiva and is a protected monument.
